Quran Verses

Important Verses with Translations and Contexts

Al-Fatiha (1:1-7)

Translation: "In the name of Allah, Most Gracious, Most Merciful..."

Context: Recited in every prayer, summarizes Islamic teachings

Ayat Al-Kursi (2:255)

Translation: "Allah - there is no deity except Him, the Ever-Living..."

Context: Powerful verse about Allah's attributes

Surah Al-Ikhlas (112:1-4)

Translation: "Say, He is Allah, [who is] One..."

Context: Describes pure monotheism
