About Islam

Islam began in the 7th century CE in Mecca with the revelation to Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him). Key aspects include:

History:

  • Revelation of Quran over 23 years
  • Spread of Islam from Arabia to worldwide
  • Major historical periods and dynasties

Practices:

  • Five daily prayers (Salah)
  • Annual fasting in Ramadan
  • Obligatory charity (Zakat)
  • Pilgrimage to Mecca (Hajj)
  • Declaration of faith (Shahada)
